# TechCorp Employee Handbook 2024

## Employee Benefits

### Health Insurance
- **Medical Coverage**: 100% premium covered for employees, 80% for family
- **Dental Plan**: Delta Dental PPO with $2,000 annual maximum
- **Vision Plan**: VSP coverage with $400 frame allowance
- **Health Savings Account**: Company contributes $1,500 annually

### Time Off Policy
- **Vacation Days**: 25 days per year (increases to 30 after 3 years)
- **Sick Leave**: 12 days per year (doesn't roll over)
- **Personal Days**: 5 floating personal days
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks paid leave for primary caregiver
- **Sabbatical**: 4-week paid sabbatical after 7 years of service

### Compensation Structure
- **Base Salary Reviews**: Annual reviews in March
- **Performance Bonus**: 10-20% of annual salary based on performance
- **Stock Options**: 1,000-5,000 shares vesting over 4 years
- **Referral Bonus**: $3,000 for successful technical hires
- **Professional Development**: $2,500 annual learning budget

### Office Locations
- **San Francisco HQ**: 1247 Market Street, Floor 12
- **Austin Office**: 301 Congress Avenue, Suite 1900
- **Remote Work**: 60% of employees work remotely
- **Co-working Stipend**: $300/month for remote employees

### Work Schedule
- **Core Hours**: 10 AM - 3 PM (all time zones)
- **Flexible Hours**: Start between 7 AM - 11 AM
- **Meeting-Free Friday**: No meetings scheduled on Fridays
- **Summer Hours**: Half-day Fridays from June to August

### Company Policies
- **Dress Code**: Casual (business casual for client meetings)
- **Equipment**: MacBook Pro M3 or Dell XPS 15 provided
- **Internet Stipend**: $100/month for home internet
- **Phone Plan**: Company-provided phone with unlimited data
- **Parking**: Free parking at all office locations

### Emergency Contacts
- **HR Hotline**: 1-800-TECHCORP (24/7 support)
- **IT Support**: support@techcorp.com or Slack #it-help
- **Building Security**: 415-555-0199 (SF), 512-555-0288 (Austin)
