# TechCorp Q3 2024 Quarterly Report

## Financial Performance

### Revenue Growth
- **Q3 2024 Revenue**: $847.2 million (18.5% increase YoY)
- **Q2 2024 Revenue**: $723.1 million
- **Q3 2023 Revenue**: $714.8 million

### Product Performance
- **CloudSync Pro**: 2.3 million active users (+45% QoQ)
- **DataVault Enterprise**: $156.7 million ARR
- **AI Assistant Platform**: 890K monthly active users

### Key Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: $127 (down from $156 in Q2)
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: $71.2 million
- **Net Revenue Retention**: 118%
- **Gross Margin**: 73.4%

### Geographic Breakdown
- **North America**: 54% of revenue ($458.1M)
- **Europe**: 31% of revenue ($262.6M)
- **Asia-Pacific**: 15% of revenue ($126.5M)

### Employee Information
- **Total Employees**: 3,847 (hiring 127 new engineers in Q4)
- **Engineering Team**: 1,234 people (32% of workforce)
- **Average Salary**: $142,000 for senior engineers

### Major Partnerships
- **Microsoft Azure Integration**: Signed $23.4M partnership deal
- **Google Cloud Platform**: Joint go-to-market strategy launched
- **Salesforce Marketplace**: Featured as Premier Partner since October 2024

### Upcoming Product Launches
- **TechCorp Mobile 3.0**: Expected launch January 15, 2025
- **Enterprise Security Suite**: Beta testing with 47 enterprise clients
- **AI Code Assistant**: Public release planned for March 2025
